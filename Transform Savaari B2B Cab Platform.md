3. Outstation services:  
   * Round Trip  
   * Round Trip Multicity  
   * Intercity One-Way transfers  
4. Holiday tour packages (vehicle with driver)  
5. Corporate employee transportation

# **Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

## **Feature: Configurable Payment Responsibility & Price Control for B2B Agents**

### **Product Context**

We are a **B2B car rental platform** built for **travel agents**.  
A key industry problem is **price opacity and payment responsibility ambiguity**, leading to:

* Agent–customer disputes  
* Driver cash collection issues  
* Vendor revenue leakage  
* Operational confusion at trip time

This feature introduces **structured payment responsibility options**, allowing vendors to control **who pays, how much, and when**, while protecting fulfillment certainty.

---

## **Objectives**

1. **Prevent price disclosure** to end customers unless explicitly intended.  
2. **Give vendors control** over payment responsibility (Agent vs Customer).  
3. **Reduce payment failures** and last-minute escalations.  
4. **Automate fallback mechanisms** to protect trip fulfillment.  
5. **Create predictable cash flow** for vendors and drivers.

---

## **Target Users**

* **Primary:** Travel Agents (B2B clients)  
* **Secondary:** Vendor Ops Team  
* **Tertiary:** Drivers (indirect impact)

---

## **Core Concept**

At the time of booking, the Agent must select **one of four mutually exclusive payment modes** using radio buttons.  
Each mode defines:

* Payment split  
* Payment timelines  
* Fallback behavior  
* Eligibility rules  
* System enforcement

---

## **Payment Modes (Radio Button Options)**

---

### **Option 1: Partial Agent Payment \+ Customer Pays Balance to Driver**

**Label (UI):**  
`25% Paid by Agent | 75% + Extras Paid by Customer to Driver`

#### **Description**

* Agent pays **25% of base fare** at booking confirmation.  
* Remaining **75% of base fare \+ all extras** (extra km, extra hours, night allowance, tolls, parking, permits, taxes, etc.) are **collected directly from the Customer by the Driver during the trip**.

#### **Rules & Constraints**

* No restriction on booking lead time.  
* Price breakup **not disclosed** to the customer in-app.  
* Driver app clearly displays:  
  * Amount to be collected  
  * Allowed payment modes (cash / UPI / wallet)

#### **System Behavior**

* Booking is confirmed only after 25% payment success.  
* Driver payout includes customer-collected amount \+ vendor settlement logic.

#### **Use Case**

* High-trust customer  
* Low agent risk appetite  
* Short notice bookings

---

### **Option 2: Full Agent Responsibility with Time-Bound Payments**

**Label (UI):**  
`25% Now | 75% by Agent 48 Hours Before Trip | Extras by Customer`

#### **Description**

* Agent pays **25% of base fare at booking**.  
* The remaining **75% must be paid by the Agent at least 48 hours before trip start time**.  
* All extras are collected directly from the Customer during the trip.

#### **Eligibility**

* Booking must be made **≥ 72 hours** before pickup time.

#### **Automated Fallback Logic**

* If the Agent **fails to pay the remaining 75% by T-48 hours**:  
  * System **automatically switches booking to Option 1**  
  * Agent is notified of the downgrade  
  * Driver is informed to collect remaining amount from Customer

#### **System Features Required**

* **Payment reminder automation**  
  * T-72h, T-60h, T-48h alerts  
* **Payment link generation**  
* **Dashboard alerts**  
  * “Bookings at Risk”  
  * “Payment Responsibility Shifted”  
* **Audit log** of payment mode changes

#### **Use Case**

* Planned itineraries  
* Agents managing multiple customers  
* Vendors seeking higher payment certainty

---

### **Option 3: Agent \+ Wallet-Based Extras Settlement**

**Label (UI):**  
`25% Now | 75% by Agent | All Extras via Wallet`

#### **Description**

* Agent pays:  
  * **25% at booking**  
  * **75% at least 48 hours before trip**  
* All extras (extra km, extra hours, night allowance, tolls, parking, permits, etc.) are settled **via Agent Wallet**.  
* Wallet can be used:  
  * During the trip, or  
  * On the last day of trip completion

#### **Eligibility**

* Agent must maintain a **Wallet balance ≥ 25% of base fare** at booking time.

#### **Wallet Rules**

* Wallet balance is **soft-locked** until trip completion.  
* Auto-deduction for approved extras.  
* Low balance alerts triggered when wallet drops below threshold.

#### **System Behavior**

* Booking fails if wallet balance criteria is not met.  
* Driver cannot collect extras directly from customer.  
* All driver extras are digitally approved.

#### **Use Case**

* Enterprise agents  
* Credit-based partners  
* Zero cash handling preference

---

### **Option 4: Full Wallet Settlement**

**Label (UI):**  
`100% Payment via Agent Wallet`

#### **Description**

* Entire amount (base fare \+ all extras) is settled through the Agent Wallet.  
* No cash or direct customer payment allowed.

#### **Rules**

* Wallet must have sufficient balance before trip start.  
* Extras are auto-debited post validation.  
* Ideal for long-term or contract agents.

#### **System Behavior**

* Hard block if wallet balance is insufficient.  
* Real-time wallet ledger and trip-wise breakdown.

#### **Use Case**

* Preferred partners  
* SLA-driven accounts  
* Zero friction operations

---

## **Cross-Cutting System Requirements**

### **1\. Price Visibility Control**

* Customer never sees:  
  * Vendor rate  
  * Agent margin  
* Only payable amount (if any) is shown contextually.

---

### **2\. Notifications & Alerts**

* Email \+ WhatsApp \+ In-app notifications:  
  * Pending payments  
  * Payment mode switches  
  * Wallet low balance  
* Driver notifications must reflect **final payment responsibility**.

---

### **3\. Audit & Compliance**

* Every payment mode selection and auto-switch must be logged.  
* Dispute-ready trail:  
  * Who selected  
  * When it changed  
  * Why it changed

---

### **4\. Failure & Edge Cases**

* Trip already started → no payment mode switching.  
* Driver app must **lock payment method** once trip starts.  
* Manual override allowed only for Admin roles.

---

* Customer-side payment UI
